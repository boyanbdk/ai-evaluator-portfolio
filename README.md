## How I work with AI

- I use tools like ChatGPT to:
  - summarise lecture notes from my university courses in Computer Science,
  - prepare for exams in subjects such as Artificial Intelligence and Logical Programming,
  - analyse macroeconomic news and economic indicators for trading,
  - support language learning (French and German).

- When I use AI, I always:
  - check the output for **factual correctness**,
  - look for **missing context or important caveats**,
  - rewrite parts of the answer to be clearer and more precise.

This mindset is very close to the responsibilities of an **AI Data Annotator** or **AI Evaluator**, where the goal is to improve AI models through high-quality feedback and well-structured data.
